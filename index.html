TIC-TAC-TOE

Goal: Get 3 of your marks in a row.

Players: 2 players, X and O.

Board: 9 squares, 3x3.

How to Play:

  • Players take turns putting their mark in empty squares.
  • First to get 3 in a row wins.

Winning:

  • Line up 3 of your marks (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal).
  • If the board fills up, it's a tie.

After the Game: Clear the board to play again.

Have fun!
